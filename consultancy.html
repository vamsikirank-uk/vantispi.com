VPI-3010 · Services

What we take on

Schedule assurance

Independent health checks of programmes before they're baselined, submitted or relied upon — DCMA-aligned, with findings you can hand straight to the contractor or the board.

Forensic delay & claims

Defensible delay analysis for extensions of time, disruption and dispute support — windows, as-planned vs as-built and time impact methods, evidenced from the programme record.

QSRA facilitation

Quantitative schedule risk analysis workshops and Monte Carlo modelling that produce P50/P80 dates a board can act on — not a spreadsheet nobody trusts.

Project controls setup

Standing up planning and controls functions for new projects and PMOs: structures, calendars, coding, progress cycles and reporting that survive contact with delivery.

Reporting & comms

Turning dense programmes into summary schedules and dashboards that boards actually read — including standing up Sketchdule-based reporting cycles.

Training

Hands-on training in schedule quality, Earned Schedule, QSRA and the Vantis toolset — pitched for working planners, not the lecture hall.

VPI-3030 · Sectors

Where we work

The methods are universal; the context isn't. We've sat on both sides of the client–contractor table in four demanding sectors.

Infrastructure

Construction

Oil & Gas
