01 · ScheduleInsight

Schedule analysis & forensic assurance

For Primavera P6 XER and MS Project files. ScheduleInsight reads your programme and returns the full assurance suite — health, logic, performance, risk and delay — as defensible, referenced reports.

  • DCMA 14-point health check — every metric, with thresholds and the activities behind each score
  • Critical path analysis — Appendix C driving chain and Appendix D delay attribution
  • Earned Schedule — SPI(t), ES, SV(t) and IEAC(t) time-based forecasting
  • Monte Carlo QSRA — P50 / P80 / P90 completion probabilities
  • Time Machine — baseline vs revision comparison, change by change
  • Delay decomposition & Claim Brief — delay typing with a one-click evidential brief
  • Multi-scenario Gantt overlay — comparison reports across revisions and what-ifs

02 · Sketchdule

Board-ready summary schedules

The gap between a 4,000-line programme and a slide the board will read is usually an evening of manual drawing. Sketchdule closes it in minutes — and keeps the summary linked to the source.

  • Native import — Primavera P6, MS Project, Excel and CSV
  • One-page summaries — milestone and Gantt views for steering committees
  • MS-Office-style interface — ribbon, right-click, shortcuts; minimal learning curve
  • Export everywhere — PDF, PowerPoint, PNG and self-contained HTML
  • Real-time co-editing — end-to-end encrypted; your data never leaves your device
