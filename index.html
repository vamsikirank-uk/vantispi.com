VPI-1010 · Why we build this way

Serious tools shouldn't need an IT ticket.

Project controls software has always meant heavyweight installs, licence servers and sending sensitive programme files to someone else's cloud. We think that's backwards.

Practitioner-built

DCMA 14-point checks, Earned Schedule, Monte Carlo QSRA, forensic delay analysis — the real methods, implemented properly by people who use them on live programmes.

Private by architecture

Everything runs client-side in your browser. Your XER, MPP and programme files are never uploaded — which is why our tools pass the strictest security reviews by default.

Zero friction

No installation, no admin rights, no procurement battle. Open a tab and work. Familiar Office-style interfaces mean your team is productive on day one.

ScheduleInsight · scheduleinsight.co.uk

Forensic schedule analysis, in minutes.

Drop in a Primavera P6 XER or MS Project file and get the analysis a claims consultant would charge weeks for — defensible, repeatable and entirely on your device.

  • DCMA 14-point — schedule health check
  • Critical path — Appendix C chain & Appendix D delay attribution
  • Earned Schedule — SPI(t), ES, SV(t), IEAC(t)
  • Monte Carlo QSRA — P50 / P80 / P90 completion probabilities
  • Time Machine — baseline vs revision comparison
  • Claim Brief — delay decomposition, ready for the record
